About me

I am Mexican born and my genuine passion for design has accompanied me on my travels to my home in Vancouver, BC. I am a colourful person and always try to think outside the box in order to create something distinct and individual. I love to be part of a fun, dedicated and positive team which can share ideas and has the ability to grow both personally and professionally. Growth as a designer is tremendously important to me as I continuously strive to push my own boundaries. Design is something I am genuinly passionate about and I pride myself with the ability to engage the user.
Every experience is a design in itself.
